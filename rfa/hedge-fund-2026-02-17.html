Methodology

Asymmetry & Propagation Methodology

Signal Detection
Equity-level opportunities identified through cybersecurity event monitoring, vulnerability disclosures, operational pattern analysis, and regulatory signal tracking. Each signal assessed for directional conviction and freshness.
Edge Classification
Edge quality classified as HIGH (contained lifecycle, strong freshness, clear asymmetry), DECAYING (spreading lifecycle, Tier-1 pickup, efficient pricing), or STALE (expired window, no residual asymmetry).
Propagation Lifecycle
Each signal tracked through lifecycle phases: Contained (operator channels only), Spreading (Tier-1 pickup beginning), Decaying (market efficiently pricing), Expired (no residual edge). Trading quality inversely correlates with propagation stage.
Market Regime
Bearish/Neutral/Bullish regime classification with crosswind risk scoring. Current regime (Bearish 62, Crosswind 68) frames execution friction and favours idiosyncratic signals over consensus trades.
