Methodology

Score and Confidence Legend

How to interpret the probability scores, direction bands, and confidence tiers used throughout this report.

Risk Probability
Assessed likelihood (0–100%) that a specific outcome will occur within the stated horizon. Higher numbers indicate stronger evidence pointing toward that outcome.
Scenario Outlook
Distribution across multiple possible pathways. Percentages represent relative likelihood of each pathway dominating; they sum to 100%.
Direction Band
Whether the risk is becoming more likely (rising), less likely (falling), or flat. Band width shows directional certainty.
Confidence Tier
Tier 1 = evidence directly addresses the question. Tier 2 = adjacent evidence requiring inference. Tier 3 = thin or indirect evidence.
